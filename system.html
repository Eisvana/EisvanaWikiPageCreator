Found on the galaxy map. Click here for a guide of how to get an accurate value. Distance to Centre In the galaxy map, move the camera very close to the star.
You must unfocus the system in order to get close enough to the star. If you're basically inside the star, that's the correct position.
You can then read the distance to centre value on the top left.

Background: This is the camera distance to centre, not the actual system distance to centre.
On PC, you can use the GalaxyCenterDistance mod to get the distance to centre accurately on the top of the galaxy map.
